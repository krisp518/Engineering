There are aeronautical engineers (planes and such) and astronautical engineers (space shuttles and more). The 4 forces of a flying object are thrust, lift, drag, and weight.   1. The final weight of our bridge was 29.9 grams.
  2. Our bridge did not hold the weight of the bucket so we started and unfortunately ended with a cup of water. This cup of water was 263.1 grams before our bridge snapped.
  3. The ratio of the weight of our bridge to the weight it carried was 29.9 : 263.1. This means our bridge held 8.8 times its weight. Though this result isn't terrible, we were expecting a higher ratio.
  4. Our bridge was equally distribitued throughout in weight with some more weight on the connected segments due to the added glue. However, we do believe a good amount of our weight came from glue rather than the spaghetti which is what should have been the primary weight. That being said, however, we do think our amount of glue wasn't entirelty too excessive.
  5. The bridge was originally inspired by truss bridges as we knew it was one of the strongest types of bridges seen in the real through research. Similiarly, we also took inspiration from tower cranes since we were already taking from the real world and we tried to reduce spaghetti usage with the triangular shape. We also knew triangles were the strongest shape which is why we followed through with this design.
  6. In some ways, the bridge worked better but in other ways it was also weaker than we had hoped for. That being said, it did reach our intended goal. Furthermore, we were in a rush and we were met with many obstacles such as snapped spaghetti in the time we were gone which happened on the last day as well. So, while we wished for better results, we are still proud of the results we were able to achieve.
  7. Our points of failure were a structurally weak middle point and an uneven distribution of weight. This was expected since we had a snapped spaghetti near the middle in our pillar and we also could have added more triangles. Furthermore, our bottom had none or hardly any triangles since we ran out of time.
  8. To improve on our bridge, we could have added more triangles, both on our main points but the point where we ddi not have any since we ran out of time to add them. We also could have added more spaghetti on our 3 connecting pillars.
